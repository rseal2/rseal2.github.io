INNOVATION PROCESSES TEACHER'S ASSISTANT AT UNL:

August 2023 - December 2023

As an Innovation Processes Teacher's Assistant, I mentored a cohort of 40 students in the Raikes School by helping them discover a problem space and work through the product development process. During this time, my teaching team and I evaluated customer interviews, prototypes, and iterations based on design feedback and business models. We helped the students use Figma to build their apps while teaching them good design practices. At the end of the semester, we helped prepare them for final presentations.

Skills Used: Figma
